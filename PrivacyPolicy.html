Privacy Policy for Note Taker

This Privacy Policy outlines how Note Taker collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information gathered from users of the Note Taker mobile application.

Information We Collect

User-Provided Information:

  • Note Taker does not collect any personal information or require users to create accounts.
  • The app allows users to create and store notes locally on their device.

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Note Taker does not automatically collect any information.

How We Use Information

  • Note Storage: The primary purpose of Note Taker is to provide users with a platform to create and store notes on their local device.

Data Security

  • Local Storage: All notes created using Note Taker are stored locally on the user's device. No data is transmitted or stored externally.

Third-Party Access

  • Note Taker does not share user data with any third parties.

Children's Privacy

  • Note Taker does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • Note Taker may update this Privacy Policy as necessary. Users are encouraged to review this page periodically for any changes.

Contact Us
